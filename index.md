<h2 style="margin: 80px 0px 10px;"></h2>

I am currently a Professor at the School of Computer Science, [Central South University](https://www.csu.edu.cn/). Previously, I served as a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at  [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/).

My research bridges **computer vision** and **deep learning systems**, with a primary focus on on-device and edge intelligence, system optimizations for resource-constrained devices, and efficient visual computing.

I earned my B.Sc., M.Sc., Ph.D. degrees all in computer science, from [Central South University](https://www.csu.edu.cn/), China, in 2016, 2018 and 2023. I was a visiting scholar at [HCS Lab](https://hcs.snu.ac.kr/), [Seoul National University](https://en.snu.ac.kr/) in 2022-2023. I also joined HEX group at Microsoft Research Asia([MSRA](https://www.msra.cn/)) from Sept. 2019 to Mar. 2020, and Institute for AI Industry Research([AIR](https://air.tsinghua.edu.cn/)), Tsinghua University from Aug. 2021 to Apr. 2022 as a research intern, respectively.
